2015 Camaro ZL1

The 2015 Camaro ZL1 is an all american muscle car. It has other rivals as in the Dodge Challenger HellCat and the Mustang. The camaros engine is a 6.2 Liter V8 it produces 580 Horse Power. The Camaro ZL1 has a 1.9-liter Roots-style supercharger taht crams air into the engine with a pair of four-lobe rotors and the aid of an intercooler.
